Our Story

Nairobi-made, made for you.

Nyaboke Smartwear started as a small tailoring corner in Nairobi and grew into a go-to wardrobe for people who want quality clothing without the guesswork. Every piece is chosen for fit, fabric, and how it makes you feel walking into a room.

  • Quality-checked before every single order ships
  • Real people to talk to — order and get help over WhatsApp
